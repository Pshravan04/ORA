The Challenge

Merging interior warmth with
exterior architecture.

The client requested a sanctuary that felt incredibly open to the stunning Miami coastline while maintaining a high degree of privacy and warmth. The challenge was integrating raw, architectural materials like concrete with soft, luxurious furnishings.

Location

Miami, Florida

Area

8,500 Sq Ft

Completed

2025

Materials

Tactile
Luxury.

We curated a palette of honed travertines, brushed brass, and bleached oak. The textures were chosen to catch the abundant natural light, creating shifting shadows throughout the day that make the space feel alive.
